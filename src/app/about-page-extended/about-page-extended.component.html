HACKATHON
BEST Mostar is organizing a Hackathon from February 21 to 23, 2025, at INTERA Technology Park. A total of 100 participants will compete in creating web applications, divided into teams of 3 to 5 members. The competition will last 35 hours, and the top 3 teams will win cash prizes. Meals and refreshments will be provided for all participants.
This project was made possible by the generous support of the American people through the United States Agency for International Development (USAID).
